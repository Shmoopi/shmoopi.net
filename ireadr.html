Speed Read Anything, Anywhere

Speed Reading Tips

Get the most out of your speed reading practice

Start Comfortable

Begin at 250-300 WPM and increase gradually by 25-50 WPM as you improve.

Practice Regularly

15 minutes daily beats one hour weekly. Consistency is key to building speed.

Choose Familiar Topics

Start with content you know well, then work up to more challenging material.
